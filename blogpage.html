Building Automatic Hand Santizer Dispenser

In this project, we built an Automatic Hand Sanitizer Dispenser to promote better hygiene using simple technology. The dispenser works without physical contact, helping to reduce the spread of germs in public places. By combining basic electronics and design, our project shows how small, practical innovations can make everyday life safer, cleaner, and more convenient for everyone.
