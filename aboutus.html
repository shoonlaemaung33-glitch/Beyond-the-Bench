About Us

Introduction to our team

Beyond the Bench is a non-profit youth organization and a youth-led initiative created by students from EDUSN Digital School that aims to change how people see , use and feel about our local parks. Instead of viewing parks as just places to sit or play, we encourages the community—especially young people—to recognize parks as living spaces where nature, culture, and history come together.

Beyond the Bench offers content focused on environmental awareness, cultural preservation, and community engagement. The platform invites users to explore events, join youth-led projects, share ideas, and volunteer in park revitalization efforts. By using creative activities, challenges, and collaboration, the initiative inspires individuals to take action and contribute to meaningful change.

Our Mission

To revitalize urban parks through community engagement and sustainable practices.

Our Vision

Creating greener, more inclusive community spaces for future generations.

Our Values

Sustainability, community, innovation, and environmental stewardship.
