Lake Winds Energy Park Wind Initiative

100 MW+ Clean Energy Generated

This award‑winning project transforms Sydney Park into a living stormwater treatment and reuse system, capturing, cleansing, and recycling runoff from the surrounding urban catchment to irrigate green spaces, sustain wetlands, and reduce potable water demand.
