Our Purpose

Our platform aims to promote environmental awareness by educating visitors about local plants, animals, and eco-friendly habits through engaging, interactive activities. It also preserves and celebrates local culture by sharing stories, artwork, and audio-visual content about the park's history. By encouraging families and children to explore and learn together, it strengthens community engagement and helps people view the park as a space for education and cultural connection.







What we offer

Environmental Awareness

Learn about native plants, local wildlife, and sustainable habits that help protect our environment. Join interactive workshops and nature walks to make a real impact!

Cultural Preservation

Discover the rich culture of our parks through festivals. Experience history firsthand and celebrate local heritage!

Community Engagement

Join hands with the community through events, volunteer projects, and collaborative programs. Together, we can make a positive difference locally!

Park Spotlight of the Month

Bayintnaung Park Enhancement Project

Hlaing-thaya Township, Yangon

This new park project is transforming the vacant land under Bayintnaung Bridge into an urban green space for recreation and health:

  • Reclaimed previously unused land under Bridges No. 1 & 2 for public use
  • Installed health-support exercise equipment and walking paths
  • Planted greenery like duck grass & star-flower trees to improve air quality
  • Plans to build sports fields and enhance water drainage systems

Frequently Asked Questions

Beyond the Bench is an interactive platform that educates visitors about local parks, promotes environmental awareness, cultural appreciation, and encourages active community engagement.

You can join local park clean-ups, attend workshops, volunteer in cultural programs, or share your own ideas to improve neighborhood parks.

Everyone! Students, teachers, families, and local residents are welcome. Programs are designed for all age groups.

The platform organizes cultural workshops, interactive storytelling, fun cultural quizzes , and showcases local festivals in parks to help visitors preserve the cultural value and meaning of our local parks.

You can submit your project ideas directly on the Project Page by filling out the submission form. Additionally, you’re welcome to share your thoughts, suggestions, or feedback in the Blog Comments section under any article. Your contributions help us make Beyond the Bench even better!
